## Hi, I'm Mobina Poulaei 👋

I'm a researcher passionate about building reliable, interpretable, and theoretically grounded AI systems. My work currently focuses on anomaly detection in histopathology images, with prior research into the robustness of Video Anomaly Detection (VAD) and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) models.

🔍 I'm deeply interested in vision–language models and text–image alignment, understanding how these modalities interact and how we can make their representations more consistent and meaningful.

🎨 I love text-to-image generation and have spent significant time exploring diffusion models, conditioning mechanisms, and the creative potential of generative systems.

📐 Beyond applied work, I'm fascinated by the theory of Machine Learning (ML). I'm actively working to deepen my understanding of theoretical ML and apply it to improve model performance or develop novel solutions to ML problems.
